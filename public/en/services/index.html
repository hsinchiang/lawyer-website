Formal Legal Demand Letter Template

We guide our clients on how to write simple formal notices. Below is the template structure:

// Formal Demand Letter Example
To Whom It May Concern:

You borrowed a principal amount of NT$10,000 from the undersigned on 
October 1, 2023, with the agreement to repay the full amount by 
October 30 of the same year. However, the said debt has not been 
repaid upon the due date.

This letter serves as a formal demand for you to settle the outstanding 
balance within seven (7) days upon receipt of this notice. 
Otherwise, legal proceedings will be initiated to protect the 
undersigned's rights and interests in accordance with the law.
