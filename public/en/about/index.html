About the Attorney

Principal Attorney: A-Bo-Bo

Ten years of dedication. Attorney A-Bo-Bo graduated from the Department of Law at National Taiwan University. Having served at major law firms and as a Law Clerk for the District Court, he possesses extensive practical experience in the legal field.

Education

  • LL.B., Department of Law, National Taiwan University
  • LL.M. (Civil Law Division), Graduate Institute of Law, National Chengchi University

Experience

  • Law Clerk, Taipei District Court
  • Junior Associate, Formosa Transnational Attorneys at Law
  • Panel Attorney, Legal Aid Foundation (LAF)
  • Committee Member, Civil Procedural Law Committee, Taipei Bar Association

Qualifications

  • Passed the National Bar Examination of the R.O.C. (Top Scorer)
  • Certified Patent Agent
  • TOEIC Gold Certificate (Score: 950)
Practice Philosophy The law is not just cold regulations, but a tool for solving problems. I strive to use the most accessible language to help clients understand their situations and advocate for their best interests.

A-Bo-Bo Law Firm We are committed to providing the most compassionate and professional legal services. Whether it’s civil disputes, criminal defense, or business contracts, we are your strongest ally.
